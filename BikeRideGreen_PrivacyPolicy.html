Bike Ride Green – Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Bike Ride Green (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not send your data to any external servers or third parties.

3. Ride Data

Ride data (such as distance, speed, elevation, calories, and intensity) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted. You may delete ride data at any time by removing the App from your device.

4. Location Access

The App requires access to your location to track rides and display routes on the map. If granted, location data is used only during your rides. If background access is allowed, the App may continue to use location while running in the background, solely to record your ride accurately. Location data is not shared or stored outside your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future if features change. Updates will be published at the same URL.
