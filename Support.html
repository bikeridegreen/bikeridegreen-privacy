❓ FAQ

Q: Does Bike Ride Green require an account?
A: No, all ride data stays on your device.

Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Yes, the app works completely offline.

Q: Does the app share my location?
A: No, your GPS data is stored locally and never shared.
