Bike Ride Green – Privacy & Safety Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Bike Ride Green (“the App”) is designed with privacy and safety in mind. Unlike many other fitness or cycling apps, Bike Ride Green does not track, profile, or share your personal data. Your information remains under your control at all times.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not require an account, does not use analytics, and does not send your data to external servers or third parties.

3. Ride Data

Ride data (such as distance, speed, elevation, calories, and intensity) is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted without your explicit choice. You may delete ride data at any time by removing the App or clearing your history within the App.

4. Location Access

The App requires access to your location to track rides and display routes on the map. Location data is used only while recording a ride. If you enable background access, it will be used solely to record your ride accurately. Location data is never shared or transmitted outside your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics tools. All calculations (such as calories, speed, and intensity) are performed locally on your device.

6. Safety Guidelines

While Bike Ride Green helps track your cycling performance, your safety always comes first. - Always keep your attention on the road. - Do not interact with the App while riding. - Use a safe mount if you display live stats. - Obey traffic laws and signals. - Respect pedestrians and fellow road users. The App is a tool to assist your cycling experience, but you are responsible for safe riding at all times.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents or guardians should supervise use of the App by minors.

8. Changes

This Privacy & Safety Policy may be updated in the future if features change. Any updates will be published at the same URL.
